FAQ

Everything you need to know

How is this different from ChatGPT?+
ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI. Getting consulting-quality output requires knowing how to prompt — structuring context, specifying domain, validating against frameworks. Calibrate handles all of that behind the scenes. You describe your situation in plain language. Five proprietary layers do the rest. And unlike ChatGPT, Calibrate’s agents execute recommendations directly — the loop closes on the platform.
My chartered accountant already advises me on strategy.+
Your CA is critical for compliance, tax, and financial reporting. Strategic consulting is a different discipline: competitive positioning, growth prioritisation, pricing design, market entry. Calibrate provides the strategic layer your CA was never trained to deliver. The two are complementary.
I already have a marketing agency.+
Calibrate doesn’t replace your agency — it makes your agency’s work more effective. Most SMBs spend on agencies without strategic direction connecting the spend. Calibrate provides that direction. Adding it is the most frictionless purchase you can make: it improves the return on spending you’re already doing.
Is my business data safe?+
Calibrate uses OAuth-based data protection. Your business data never becomes training material for general-purpose models. This is an architectural decision, not a policy.
What languages are supported?+
Hindi and English at launch. Tamil, Marathi, Gujarati, Telugu, Kannada on the expansion roadmap. Calibrate’s multilingual capability processes business context at the semantic level — recommendations are natively generated, not translated.
Will this replace my consultant?+
No. Calibrate delivers entry-to-mid-level consultant quality — transformative for the 95% of SMBs that have never had any strategic guidance. For deeper advisory, the platform connects you with human strategists. We amplify consultants. We don’t replace them.
